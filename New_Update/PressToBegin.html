Bus Ticketing Kiosk: Quick Guide

Welcome! This kiosk helps you easily purchase bus tickets, reload transit cards, or check schedules. Whether you're commuting daily or visiting Calgary for the first time, here’s how to navigate the system.

How to Use the Kiosk

  1. Press “Start” on the welcome screen
    Choose your action: Buy Tickets, Reload, or View Info.
  2. Select Ticket Type
    Choose from single-use, round-trip, monthly, or yearly passes.
  3. Choose Your User Group
    Adult, Senior, Student, or Minor.
  4. Set Quantities
    Use + / – to select quantity.
  5. Review Cart
    Double-check your selections.
  6. Make Payment
    Use cash or card. Follow on-screen instructions.
  7. Collect Ticket(s)
    Take your ticket(s) and receipt.
  8. Done?
    Tap “Cancel” or wait — it resets automatically.

Helpful Tips

  • Tourists: Change language on the welcome screen.
  • Students: Enter student ID for discounts.
  • Groups: Buy multiple tickets at once.
