Bus Ticketing Kiosk: Quick Guide

This kiosk helps you easily purchase tickets, reload transit cards, or check schedules. Here’s how to navigate:

  1. Start: Choose what you’d like to do.
  2. Ticket Type: Single-use, round-trip, monthly, or yearly.
  3. User Group: Adult, Senior, Student, or Minor.
  4. Payment: Cash or card.
  5. Done: Collect ticket & receipt. Kiosk auto-resets after inactivity.
  • Tourists: Switch language on the home screen.
  • Students: Use your student ID for discounts.
  • Groups: Buy multiple tickets in one session.
