Bus Ticketing Kiosk: Quick Guide

This screen allows you to select how many tickets you’d like to purchase based on the type of rider.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Identify your rider type: Choose between Adult (18–65), Senior (65+), Student (with ID), or Minor (under 18).
  2. Adjust the quantity: Use the ( + ) button to add tickets or ( – ) to reduce the count.
  3. Watch your total: The Total Amount at the bottom will automatically update based on your selections.
  4. Proceed: Once you’ve selected all the needed tickets, click "Add to Cart" or "Pay" depending on your next step.

Helpful Tips:

  • Click the “?” button anytime to open this guide again.
  • If you're not sure about pricing, totals are calculated based on city-standard rates shown per user group.
  • Click “Add to Cart” if you wish to continue adding more ticket types.
  • Click “Pay” to go directly to payment.
