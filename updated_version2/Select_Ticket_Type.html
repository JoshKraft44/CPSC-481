Bus Ticketing Kiosk: Quick Guide

Welcome! This kiosk helps you easily purchase bus tickets, reload transit cards, or check schedules. Here's how:

  1. Start: Choose an action: Ticket, Pass, Schedule, Map.
  2. Type: Choose single-use or round-trip.
  3. User Group: Adult, Senior, Student, Minor.
  4. Payment: Follow the instructions on screen.
  5. Done: Collect ticket and receipt. The kiosk will reset after a few minutes of inactivity.
